CMake常用指令

2023-07-24 1 min read # CMake

1.指定安装路径

-DCMAKE_INSTALL_PREFIX:PATH=/path/to/install

2.添加包路径

export PKG_CONFIG_PATH=/root/workProject/hardware-3rd-linux-x86/libusb/:$PKG_CONFIG_PATH    
#注意该路径需要有 libusb.pc文件

3.添加c++ FPIC参数

-DCMAKE_POSITION_INDEPENDENT_CODE=ON
